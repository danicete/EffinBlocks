What the Eff' is Curse Blocks Anyways?

Front-end development is sometimes a messy affair. The tools and methods in our toolkit were designed to be easy to learn and execute. Unfortunately, that inherited simplicity favors mistakes. Large mistakes. As projects grow in complexity, the limits of traditional HTML & CSS become apparent and begin to bottleneck development. CSS organization, maintenance, and extensibility become a developer's worst nightmare. Markup is injected with a multitude of hacks and helper elements that eventually form a cryptic layer of disjointed ideas over the content. Combine all these shortcomings and you've officially created a living, breathing monster on the web that feeds off helpless designers and developers.

Curse Blocks is a proposed solution to these problems. Curse Blocks is not a framework but rather a repository of established practices that help speed up the time of development and guarantee quality production straight out of the box. Existing frameworks guide you down a set path; Curse Blocks promotes the freedom to choose, modify, and create.
